You have been invited to take part in a research study to read and rate different statements. This study will be supervised by Prof. Ulf J.J. Hahnel, Faculty of Psychology, University of Basel, Switzerland and Zahra Rahmani Azad, Faculty of Psychology, University of Basel.

If you agree to participate in this study, you will be asked to do the following:

  • Complete a set of online questionnaires. You may be asked to rate how well certain statements or personality traits describe you.
  • You will also be asked to report some demographic information, including your age, gender, political partisanship, and income.
  • You will make several decisions between two options with different monetary outcomes. Your bonus payoff for this study depends on the choices you will make in each trial, and may vary.

Duration: Participation in this study will require approximately 10 to 15 minutes of your time.

Risks and Benefits: There are no known risks associated with your participation in this research beyond those of everyday life. You will be incentivized by Prolific for taking part in this study. Your participation may help to better understand open research questions in behavioral and environmental psychology. Your reimbursement will involve a fixed payment of £1.50 (which equals to around USD $1.87). On top of that, depending on your choices during the study, you may or may not get an additional bonus payment of up to £1.10 (which equals to around USD $1.37).

Final Payoff = Fixed Amount + Bonus

Data Protection: Confidentiality of your data records will be strictly maintained by assigning code numbers to each participant so that data is never directly linked to individual identity. No one, including the researchers of this study, will be able to reveal your identity. The data will be made available in an anonymized form on Open Science platforms allowing other researchers to use the data for their research purposes. All sensitive information that might potentially be used to reveal your identity will be removed from the dataset prior to publishing the data. We, the researchers on the project, will also never know the identity of any of the participants, unless you will reveal it to us by contacting us via mail.

Participation in this study is voluntary. You may refuse to participate or withdraw at any time without penalty. You will only receive a payment for participation if you complete the study all the way to the end.
