On the next pages, you will find two boxes. Each box contains a number of statements about climate change coming from different sources.

When you click on one of the boxes, one statement will be randomly drawn from that box. Your task will be to read the statement and rate what you think about the statement. You will repeat this for 15 rounds. In each round, you can decide from which box you want to receive the new statement.

Similar to media analytics for online content, we are interested which box receives more attention and what you think about the statements. Please take your time to read each statement carefully and give your honest opinion.
