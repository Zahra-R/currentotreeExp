This is how a carbon footprint of an individual looks like in our task.


The different lifestyles these individuals have refer to:
  • Dieraty behavior: The respective person either followa a meat-based diet (omnivorous) or a plant-based diet (vegetarian)
  • Recycling behavior: The respective person either recycle comprehensively or does not recycle
  • Regionality of food purchases: The respective person either buys and consumes mainly regional food or also imported food items
  • Electricity supply: The respective person either subscribed to an electricity supply that is conventional, fossil based but includes some renewable source or based 100% on renewable energy sources
  • Commuting to work: The respective person either commutes a total of 20km to and from work everyday, 5 days a week for 48 weeks per year by bike or by bus
  • Traveling on vacation: The respective person either takes two medium distance flights a year (=3-6hours) or never flies to go on vacation

Next, you will estimate the carbon footprint of 16 persons with different lifestlyes.
Please look at each individual carbon footprint carefully and take some time to come up with an evaluation.
The order of presentation of the behaviors will change across the 16 examples you will see.
